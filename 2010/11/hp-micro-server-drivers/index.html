HP Micro Server Drivers

A friend of mine called me to ask if I knew the link to the page where he could download the drivers for his new HP Micro Server, one would assume that if you click on a link called HP Support & Drivers it would take you to the correct page, but no!. And calling HP didn’t help. While it’s not impossible to find the drivers, HP could have made a better job. For later reference this is the correct link for those who need to download the drivers for Windows or Linux. http://h20000.www2.hp.com/bizsupport/TechSupport/DriverDownload.jsp?prodNameId=4310887&lang=en&cc=us&prodTypeId=15351&prodSeriesId=4248009&taskId=135
